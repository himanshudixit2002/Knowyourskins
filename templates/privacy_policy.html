Our Commitment to Your Privacy

At KNOWYOURSKINS, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our AI-powered dermatology services. Please read this policy carefully to understand our practices regarding your personal data.

1. Information We Collect

We collect personal information you provide when using our skin analysis services. This information helps us deliver personalized dermatological assessments and recommendations.

Account Information

  • Name and contact details (email, phone number)
  • Account credentials and authentication information
  • Profile information (age, gender, skin concerns)

Skin Analysis Data

  • Skin images uploaded for analysis
  • AI-generated analysis results and metrics
  • Historical skin condition tracking data

Appointment Information

  • Scheduling details and preferences
  • Consultation history and notes
  • Specialist selection and availability

Payment Information

  • Payment method details (processed securely via third-party providers)
  • Billing address and transaction history
  • Subscription status and preferences

Automatically Collected Information

In addition to the information you provide, we automatically collect certain information when you use our services, including:

  • Device Information: Type of device, operating system, browser type, screen resolution, and IP address.
  • Usage Data: How you interact with our services, including pages visited, features used, and time spent.
  • Location Information: General location based on IP address or more precise location if you grant permission.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for various purposes to provide, maintain, and improve our services. Here's how we use your data:

Service Provision

  • Provide personalized skin health analysis and recommendations
  • Process and manage appointment bookings with dermatologists
  • Facilitate secure payment processing for premium services

Service Improvement

  • Improve our AI analysis algorithms and accuracy
  • Develop new features and services based on user needs
  • Analyze usage patterns to enhance user experience

Communication

  • Send appointment confirmations and reminders
  • Provide important service updates and notifications
  • Respond to your inquiries and support requests

Security & Compliance

  • Ensure secure transactions and prevent fraud
  • Verify identity and authenticate account access
  • Comply with legal obligations and regulatory requirements

3. Information Sharing

We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. We do not sell your personal data to third parties. However, we may share your information in the following circumstances:

Service Providers

We share information with trusted third-party service providers who perform services on our behalf, such as payment processing, data analysis, email delivery, hosting services, and customer service. These providers have access to your personal information only to perform these tasks on our behalf and are obligated not to disclose or use it for any other purpose.

Healthcare Professionals

When you book an appointment through our platform, we share relevant information with the selected dermatologist or healthcare professional to facilitate your consultation. This may include your skin analysis results, concerns, and medical history you've provided.

Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required to do so by law or in response to valid requests by public authorities (e.g., a court or government agency). We may also disclose your information to protect our rights, privacy, safety, or property, or that of our users or others.

Business Transfers

If KNOWYOURSKINS is involved in a merger, acquisition, or sale of all or a portion of its assets, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction. We will notify you via email and/or a prominent notice on our website of any change in ownership or uses of your personal information.

Anonymized & Aggregated Data

We may share anonymized, aggregated data that does not identify any individual user for various purposes, including:

  • Research and development of dermatological treatments and technologies
  • Industry analysis, benchmarking, and trend identification
  • Academic publications and educational materials

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, and destruction. Our comprehensive security approach includes:

Encryption

SSL/TLS encryption for all data transfers between your device and our servers, ensuring your information remains private during transmission.

Access Controls

Strict access controls and authentication mechanisms to ensure only authorized personnel can access your data on a need-to-know basis.

Regular Audits

Periodic security audits and penetration testing to identify and address potential vulnerabilities in our systems.

Data Breach Notification

In the unlikely event of a data breach that compromises your personal information, we will notify you promptly in accordance with applicable laws. This notification will include information about the breach, the data affected, and steps you can take to protect yourself.

While we implement these security measures, please be aware that no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. We strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your personal information, but we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

5. Your Rights

We respect your control over your personal information. Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your data:

Access and Portability

You have the right to access and download a copy of your personal data that we hold about you. You can view most of this information directly in your account settings.

Correction

You have the right to update or correct any inaccurate information we have about you. Most information can be updated directly through your account settings.

Deletion

You have the right to request deletion of your personal data under certain circumstances. You can delete your account and associated data from the account settings page or by contacting our support team.

Restriction and Objection

You have the right to restrict or object to our processing of your personal data under certain circumstances. You can manage your preferences in your account settings.

Opt-Out of Marketing

You have the right to opt-out of marketing communications at any time. You can unsubscribe using the link in our emails or update your communication preferences in your account settings.

How to Exercise Your Rights

To exercise any of these rights, you can:

  • Access your account settings through our website or mobile app
  • Contact our privacy team at privacy@knowyourskins.com
  • Submit a request through our support portal

We will respond to all legitimate requests within 30 days. Occasionally, it may take us longer if your request is particularly complex or you have made multiple requests. In this case, we will notify you and keep you updated.

6. Cookies & Tracking

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your experience on our website and app. These technologies help us understand how you interact with our services, remember your preferences, and improve our offerings.

Essential Cookies

These cookies are necessary for the website to function properly. They enable basic functions like page navigation, secure areas access, and account authentication. The website cannot function properly without these cookies.

Analytics Cookies

These cookies help us understand how visitors interact with our website by collecting and reporting information anonymously. They help us improve our website and services based on user behavior.

Functional Cookies

These cookies enable enhanced functionality and personalization, such as remembering your preferences, language choices, and login information for future visits.

Marketing Cookies

These cookies track your online activity to help advertisers deliver more relevant advertising or to limit how many times you see an ad. They can share this information with other organizations or advertisers.

Cookie Management

You can manage your cookie preferences through our cookie consent banner when you first visit our website. You can also modify these settings at any time by clicking on "Cookie Settings" in the footer of our website.

Most web browsers allow you to control cookies through their settings preferences. However, limiting cookies may affect the functionality of our website.
